# Audience

Who you serve, write for, or work with — your agent reads this before drafting content, emails, or offers so the tone and substance land with the right people. The italicized lines are examples; replace them with your own answers. If you haven't defined an audience yet, write down what you know today and refine it later.

## Who is my audience?

*Solo founders and small agency owners, mostly non-technical, who want to use AI tools in their daily work but don't have time to follow every new release.*

## Their typical problems

*Too many tools and no system connecting them; notes scattered across apps; they start strong with new systems and abandon them after two weeks.*

## Their goals

*A simple, reliable workflow they actually stick with — and content that explains AI without hype or jargon.*

## How I help them

*Practical, tested setups and honest write-ups: what worked, what broke, and what it costs.*
