# Welcome to your inbox

This is your inbox — the front door for anything that doesn't have a home yet. Capture whatever is on your mind here: a quick thought, a link, a half-formed idea, a task, a voice note transcript, without worrying about where it should eventually live or how it should be structured. At the start of every session, your agent checks this folder and offers to triage what's here, sorting it into projects, areas, resources, or the archive. You don't need to organize anything yourself — just get it out of your head and into this folder. Try it right now: add one thought below this line, then start a new session with your agent and watch it offer to help you sort it out.
